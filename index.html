What Are You Going to Do Today?

If you're looking for me in realspace, I can be found worshipping at Central Christian Center. When not engaged there or at work, I might be at home, or I might be up at Hurley's Heroes pushing varying types of wargaming miniatures around a table. If you're a gamer in the Joplin area, look up Joplin Wargaming on Facebook. If you're new or live away from the area, remember to send that info in a note; we don't want to exclude anyone, but spam bots and trolls are real. Speaking of things in cyberspace, I play digital games on Xbox Live and Steam. I can be found lurking around the social media scene on Facebook, , Twitter, LinkedIn, and if you aren't in hurry for a response, I check my email with varying regularity.

I've also started working with projects over on Github, which brings me to the point of this page. This page is used as a testing and development area, as well as a place to demonstrate examples of my work moving forward. Things tend to come in bursts because I'm quite busy with a number of projects. I've found that being engaged in things makes it harder to write about those things. So - if the page is quiet for a while, I'm still around.

There are going to be some new content sections coming to the site. I'm going to try to work out a system to get my blog off of Wordpress. I don't have anything against them, but I'd like to keep everything in one spot. I'm adding pages for projects and portfolio examples. Ongoing items will be in kept in projects, while finished works will go in the portfolio - that may change in the future, but it's what I'm going with for now.
